About this calculator

The TG-CCF Pediatric eGFR Calculator estimates pediatric eGFR from age, height, and serum creatinine. When local measured GFR calibration samples are available, the tool can apply center-specific intercept calibration.

The model was developed using data from five Chinese hospitals. The overall median measured GFR was 110.2 mL/min/1.73 m², indicating that the development population had largely preserved kidney function. Results should therefore be interpreted cautiously in populations with severe kidney impairment or clinical profiles outside the model development range.

Patient eligibility

  • Children and adolescents aged 0–18 years.
  • Height must be 63–189 cm.
  • Serum creatinine must be 9–273.1 μmol/L, or 0.10–3.09 mg/dL.
  • Values outside these ranges are unavailable in this prototype.

Ages below 0.33 years are allowed by the public age range but fall below the lower spline boundary used by the model and will be flagged.

Restricted research prototype. This prototype is intended for use by clinicians or researchers for research display and external validation support. Patients or caregivers should not upload patient-level data by themselves. It should not replace clinical judgment or be used as a standalone medical decision-making tool. All calculations are performed locally in the browser; no patient-level data are uploaded or stored by this static webpage.
